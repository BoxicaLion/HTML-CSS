Marine Biology

The composition of SeaWater

Almost anything can be found in seawater. This include dissolved materials form
Earth's crust as well as materials released from organisms. The most important
components of seawater that influence life forms are salinity, temperature
dissolved gases (most oxygen and carbon dioxide), nutrients, and PH. These
elements vary in their composition as well as in their influence on marine life.
